Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small local meetup for developers and tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has evolved into a globally recognized conference attracting thousands of attendees, innovators, and thought leaders from across the technology sector.

Our Mission

TechCon aims to inspire and empower the global tech community through knowledge sharing, innovation showcases, and community building. We believe in inclusive technology for all and prioritize education, collaboration, and ethical advancement of tech.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Amina Rahman

A renowned AI researcher and ethics advocate, Dr. Rahman spoke about responsible AI development and the future of machine learning in society.

Jason Lee

Founder of Code4Change, Jason shared insights on using open source tools to solve global challenges and empower underserved communities.

A pioneer in quantum computing, Professor Zhang presented groundbreaking research and participated in a panel on future tech education.
